“As one Person I cannot change the world,but I can change the world of one person’’ ~ Paul Shane Spear


NGO aimed at providing a platform for young individuals trying to contribute towards society and change the world because small steps make huge differences. The need of the hour is to make communities aware of their rights.Educated communities will not just create better citizens but also ensure better employment and enterprise.And our main motive is to impart Education and spread and share the khushiyan and privileges that we are blessed with.

We believe that an investment in knowledge pays best interests.Teaching Children is an accomplishment but getting children excited about learning while playing is an achievement.


Our vision is to increase awareness about importance of education in shaping and improving quality of life.Our long term vision is to have presence in all the cities of India by building strong community of individuals excited to bring change.And bridge gap between slums and schools.
