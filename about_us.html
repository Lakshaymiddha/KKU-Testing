Our Story So Far

It started from the campus of Delhi University where a socially aware group of students used to teach,play, laugh and spend time with underprivileged kids receiving overwhelming joy ,satisfaction and peace. After spending time with them we realised how pure their emotions are and to show their gratitude and love they used to give us chocolates and candy from their own money.
